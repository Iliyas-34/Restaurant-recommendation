Data Schemas

  "Restaurant Name": string,

Authentication Flow

Users sign up or log in at /auth (or split pages), the session is established with session['user'] and used for profile and user-specific routes.

Admin Controls

Basic review management at /admin/reviews with DELETE /review/<id> for moderation.
